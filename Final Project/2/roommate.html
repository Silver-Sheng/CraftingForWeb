Roommate (2020)

This is a 9 pages fiction genre screenplay.

The main protagonists are two college students who live in the same apartment. They are both jealous of each other's lives in some way, like family relationships and living attitude. They never show this side to each other. One day, during dinner, they find out they both have something that the other wants.
