Internet Addiction (2020)

This is a 18 pages fiction genre screenplay.

The story happens in an Internet Addiction help center. In the first meeting, a rebellious young man interrupts the treatment process for two other patients and stands in a position against the therapist. This young man believes that focusing one's life online is not a problem. In the second meeting, the young man and therapist argue about why do people have to trouble themselves to fit in a real-life community with limited culture while the communities and culture are more diverse and compatible. The young man makes an extreme example and put himself in a difficult situation. In the third meeting, the therapist asks him to open up with his story and finds out about his childhood experience.
