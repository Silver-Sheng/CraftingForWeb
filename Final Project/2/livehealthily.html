#LiveHealthily (2019)

Project Description:
#LiveHealthily is a short film inspired by the lifestyle of the younger generation. They live a "double life" - in social media and real life. Some people tend to establish a more positive image of themselves online, which is mostly different than who they are in real life, to gain attention and accomplishment from the internet.
