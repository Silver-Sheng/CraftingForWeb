Context

“100 Anti-theses on Cyberfeminism” is organized by Old Boys Network.
Old Boys Network(OBN) is the first international cyberfeminist alliance that was founded in Berlin, Germany in 1997. It active from 1997 to 2001.
The First Cyberfeminist International agreed on not to define the term. The strategy of keeping the term as open as possible was consensual. As a substitute for a definition The First Cyberfeminist International formulated the "100 Anti-heses" (reference)


What was it pushing back against and advocate for?

“100 Anti-theses on Cyberfeminism” is a manifesto that aims at defining what Cyberfeminism is not as the First Cyberfeminist International agreed on not to define the term cyberfeminism.
As Guertin says that cyberfeminism is a celebration of multiplicity. This manifesto critically discourses the concept of cyberfeminism and advocate not limiting the definitions and possibilities for cyberfeminism.
