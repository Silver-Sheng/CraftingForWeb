Chocolate-flavored Desserts/Snacks

I am a big fan of chocolate-flavored desserts and snacks, such as chocolate cake, chocolate ice cream, chocolate cookies, and chocolate mochi. I specifically like eating chocolate desserts and snacks when I am having a bad mood. This is one way for me to release my stress, which makes me curious about why I will feel better when eating chocolate desserts. After searching on the internet, I learned that Chocolate contains a small amount of tryptophan , an amino acid, which is linked to the production of serotonin , which is a neurotransmitter that produces feelings of happiness. Besides, eating something sweet can cause a surge in dopamine by going quickly from the stomach to the bloodstream and then to the brain. Dopamine is the hormone that causes feelings of happiness, excitement, and joy.
