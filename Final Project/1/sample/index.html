Death of Sulli

In 2019, The death of Sulli, a South Korean actress, is one of the most representative cyberbullying incidents.

Sulli was cheerful and cute as a child star. Sulli's family was not wealthy, so her mother sent her to a performance arts school.

The overall environment of online society is much more hostile and negative than in the real world. Many netizens start to judge or comment on others on social media before they fully understand what happened.

In most cases, they unscrupulously make judgemental comments and spread malice to others due to the herd mentality, impulse, and the anonymous nature of the Internet. This phenomenon is referred to as cyberbullying. They lost the ability to empathize with the victim under such irrational conditions. Such behavior would lead to severe consequences to the victim such as depression, Irreversible psychological damage, loss of privacy and normal daily life, and even suicide.
