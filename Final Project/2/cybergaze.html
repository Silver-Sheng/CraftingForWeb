CYBERGAZE (2021)

Cyber violence is a rising conflict that cannot be ignored. Many netizens judge or comment on othersâ€™ social media posts before they fully understand what happened. Such behavior would negatively impact the victim and could lead to serious consequences. This installation art aims to simulate the pressure that a victim is experiencing on the other side of the screen and project it to the viewer.
