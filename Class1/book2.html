I Used to Have A Plan: But Life Had Other Ideas

This book is a memoir with a lot of illustrations, which somehow brings me back to my childhood when reading picture books. I resonate with the drawing and its use of language a lot and learn a new perspective and attitude for facing the downside of life. This book has a magic healing power that allows me to slow down the pace of my life and take time to truly face the negative emotions that hid deep down my heart.
