I Used to Have A Plan: But Life Had Other Ideas

Reading paper books is another way I like to use to to release my stress. This traditional medium has the power for clearing out distractions and organizing my mind. The minute I hold a book in my hand, the woody and the lovely smell of the book would calm me down. The book I Used to Have A Plan: But Life Had Other Ideas helped me go through a difficult time in my life. It gave me a great sense of comfort when I struggled with several chaotic changes happened in my life. This book is a memoir with a lot of illustrations, which somehow brings me back to my childhood when reading picture books. I resonate with the drawing and its use of language a lot and learn a new perspective and attitude for facing the downside of life. This book has a magic healing power that allows me to slow down the pace of my life and take time to truly face the negative emotions that hid deep down my heart.
