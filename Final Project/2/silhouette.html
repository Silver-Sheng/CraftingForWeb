Silhouette Animation (2021)

Project Description:
Medium: Cutout 2D animation
Software: After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop
Style: silhouette, Victorian style, thick outline

Overview:
It starts from a little boy’s childhood experience. His mom is mean to him after his sister passed away. He tries many ways to behave well to get his mom's approval and care, but all his mom did is scolding him. The only time that his mom hugs here are when he dresses up as his sister. Throughout his early life, the little boy hides in the closet whenever he feels unsafe. After he grows into a teenager, he misses the only time that he could get someone’s attention. He begins to secretly try on his mom's dress and appreciate his beauty in front of the mirror. His mom finds out about his secret hobby and scolds him. Later, his mom can’t stand him anymore and leaves him. Since then, he releases himself from his mom’s control and starts to dress like a female, and makes lots of new friends. He lives as a normal person happily until one day he receives his mom’s gift during his birthday.
