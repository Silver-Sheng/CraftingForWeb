Social Life (2018)

Project Description:
Social Life is a short film project inspired by some of my friends around me. Sometimes when I hang out with my friends, we would constantly stare at our phones rather than talking to each other. Sometimes people put more effort into relationship building online but forgetting the value of face-to-face connection.
