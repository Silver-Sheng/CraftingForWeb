Why I Never Give Up Drawing

Drawing is one of my favorite and most efficient way to relax and release stress. When I feel overwhelmed by school, work or people, I will start drawing something to distract myself from real life or sometimes escape from the mess in my life. I am always easily distracted by things around me, except when I am drawing. I enjoy every moment of drawing and how I am able to pay full attention to it. I feel safe and comfortable while drawing. This process would help me clear the negative energy in my mind and calm down. It helps me able to organize my thoughts and face problems later. (A random fun fact: there is a phrase in Chinese called three-minutes passion, which is use for describe this kind of personality. I found this is quite an accurate and interesting expression.)
