Feel My Life (2021)

Project Description:
Alzheimer’s disease is one of the most common causes of death for the elderly in the United States in the past several years. However, the public lacks awareness of this progressive and incurable disease. sThis immersive experience activity is meant to help people to get a better understanding of Alzheimer’s disease with several visual, auditory and verbal experiences.
