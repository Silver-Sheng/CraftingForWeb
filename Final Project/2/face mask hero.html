Face Mask Hero (2021)

Project Description:
Children from age three to five have not yet developed a mature immune system. Also, they are usually reluctant to put on uncomfortable physical protection. Children face a bigger threat from disease than adults do. This pop-up book is aimed at helping children to understand complex health problems in an interactive and simple way and learn how to protect themselves from the pandemic.
