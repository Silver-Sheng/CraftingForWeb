I Used to Have A Plan: But Life Had Other Ideas

Reading paper books is another way I like to use to to release my stress. This traditional medium has the power for clearing out distractions and organizing my mind. The minute I hold a book in my hand, the woody and the lovely smell of the book would calm me down. The book I Used to Have A Plan: But Life Had Other Ideas helped me go through a difficult time in my life. It gave me a great sense of comfort when I struggled with several chaotic changes happened in my life.
